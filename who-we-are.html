ABOUT US

IMODELS is a small modeling agency established in early 2016 in Vietnam. Our agency aims to lead the Vietnam modeling industry into a new era of promise and growth by searching for young talented faces inside and outside Vietnam. Its aim is to train and develop successful young faces in the fashion world.

We own a professional team filled with enthusiasm and passion that will help and create great opportunities for young talents to increase their income and build their reputation in the world.
